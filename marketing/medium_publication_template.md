## Tag Selection Guidelines

Choose tags that:
1. Accurately describe your content
2. Have active readership (check tag follower counts)
3. Mix broad appeal with niche specificity
4. Relate to your target audience

## Subtitle Best Practices

- Keep it under 140 characters
- Expand on the title without repeating it
- Focus on the reader's benefit or outcome
- Include keywords for SEO
- Make it scannable and compelling

## Featured Image Guidelines

- Dimensions: 1400 x 788 pixels (or similar 16:9 ratio)
- High quality, professional appearance
- Relevant to the content
- Can be a diagram from your article
- Avoid text-heavy images (Medium adds title overlay)
