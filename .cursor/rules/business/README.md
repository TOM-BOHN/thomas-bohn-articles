# Business Rules

Rules for business value analysis, prioritization, and training when planning or delivering documentation and article work.

## Cluster

- **value-analyst** — Value chain, VRIO, SWOT for doc work. Use when assessing strategic impact of a doc/article initiative.
- **priority-analyst** — RICE and impact/effort prioritization. Use when deciding what to do first or whether to proceed.
- **training-expert** — Backwards-design training for docs and workflows. Use when creating training or enablement materials.

## When to use which

- Assessing business value or strategy → **value-analyst**
- Prioritizing work or making go/no-go decisions → **priority-analyst**
- Designing training or rollout materials → **training-expert**
